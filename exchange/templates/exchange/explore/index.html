Questions

What's this all about?

I made a site for us to play a round of networked secret santa. Maybe I'll put other things on here later. It's a great domain name, right?

Why?

Too many of my friends live far away now, and I can't afford gifts for everyone anyways, but I still wanted to cheer you up. Cards Against Humanity dug a giant hole to celebrate the season. My inner nihilist loves that, and perversely enough, it's inspired me to do something nice.

Who's this for?

Friends and friends of friends. We'll see how big it gets. Don't have an invite code but want to participate? Ask for one.

When's it over?

The first round is December 2016. I'll send everyone a summary of how it went after New Year's. If this works, we'll keep it going.

Couldn't you achieve this through a facebook group?

Probably, except I don't use facebook. But I code and I love you. <3

It's broken.

The site, your faith in humanity, or the world? The first maybe I can fix, the second maybe you can, and the third... well maybe there's a decent chance of improvement if more of us try. No guarantees. Email space.possibility@gmail.com
